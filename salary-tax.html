Old vs New Tax Regime

The government has introduced a New Tax Regime with lower tax rates but fewer deductions. The Old Tax Regime allows you to claim exemptions like HRA, LTA, and deductions under Section 80C, 80D, etc.

Which one should you choose?

  • Choose Old Regime if: You have significant investments in PPF, LIC, ELSS (Section 80C) and pay rent (HRA).
  • Choose New Regime if: You prefer simplicity, have fewer investments, or your income falls in lower brackets where defaults work better.

Common Deductions

  • Section 80C: Up to $1,500 (example limit) for investments in PF, Insurance, etc.
  • Section 80D: Premiums paid for health insurance for self and family.
  • Standard Deduction: A flat deduction allowed for all salaried employees.
