Credit Card Debt: The Silent Killer

Credit cards are convenient, but the interest rates (APR) can be staggering—often between 20-40%. If you are unable to pay the full balance, converting it to an EMI at a lower rate (usually 13-18%) can be a smart move.

EMI vs Revolving Credit

When you pay only the "Minimum Due," you are stuck in revolving credit which attracts the highest interest charges. Converting to EMI sets a fixed schedule to become debt-free.

Hidden Charges

Before opting for an EMI, watch out for:

  • Processing Fee: Usually 1% to 2% of the transaction value.
  • Foreclosure Charges: If you want to repay early, banks might charge a penalty (up to 3%).
  • GST: Applicable on interest and fees.
