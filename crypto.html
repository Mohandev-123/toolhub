Calculating Crypto Gains

Cryptocurrency markets are highly volatile. Accurate calculation of profits and losses is essential not just for portfolio tracking but also for tax purposes.

Understanding Fees

Exchanges charge fees on both buy and sell orders. Even a small 0.1% fee can eat into profits if you trade frequently (scalping). Always factor these in.

Realized vs Unrealized Gains

Unrealized Gains: Paper profits. The value of your portfolio has gone up, but you haven't sold yet.

Realized Gains: You have sold your crypto for fiat (USD/EUR) or another crypto. This is a taxable event in many jurisdictions.

HODLing vs Trading

HODLing (Holding On for Dear Life) is a passive strategy where you buy and hold for years. Trading involves frequent buying and selling to capture short-term market movements. Each has different risk profiles and tax implications.
